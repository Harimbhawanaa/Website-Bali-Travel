Garuda Wisnu Kencana

A Vision Carved in Copper and Brass

About Garuda Wisnu Kencana

A monumental cultural icon in Bali featuring a massive statue of Lord Vishnu riding the mythical bird, Garuda. Standing taller than the Statue of Liberty, this masterpiece is crafted from copper and brass, symbolizing spiritual mission and environmental protection.
